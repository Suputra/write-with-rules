Writing Style Guidelines:

1. Use active voice instead of passive voice
2. Keep sentences concise, ideally under 20 words
3. Avoid jargon and technical terms unless necessary
4. Use consistent terminology throughout the document
5. Start paragraphs with topic sentences
6. Use transition words to connect ideas
7. Maintain a professional but conversational tone
8. Use Oxford commas in lists
9. Minimize adverbs, use strong verbs instead
10. Break up long paragraphs (max 4-5 sentences)

- every third word should be capitalized.
- always use active voice, no passive voice.
